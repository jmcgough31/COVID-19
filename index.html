COVID-19 Daily Blog

04 08 2020

U.S reported cases: 396,223 +8%
U.S reported deaths: 12,722 +18%
Virginia reported cases: 3335 +16%

The U.S overall daily growth rate continues to drop slowly. We only saw 8% new cases yesterday. This trend continues to follow the prediction that the U.S will hit peek mid to end of April.   

The U.S did see a large spike in the daily death growth rate. From 12% to 18%. Unfortunately, this jump brings the case fatality rate up to 3.2%

Virginia also saw a large jump in the daily growth rate 9% to 16% with 457 new case.  This could be due to delayer reporting.  Virginia has had reported errors before. Hopefully we will see a big drop tomorrow. 

04 07 2020

U.S reported cases: 366,614 +9%
U.S reported deaths: 10,783 +12%
Virginia reported cases: 2,878 +9%

Today the downward daily growth trend continues in all the areas.

For reported cases the rate we see now is the lowest it has ever been at 9%.

The daily growth rate for deaths has also turned downward steeply from last week. Today we only saw 12% compared to last week when the percentage was in the mid 20s. however the case fatality rate to continues to tick slightly upward(2.9%) due to the higher death daily growth rate. Still much lower than most of Europe.

Virginia is continuing to follow the same downward daily growth trend with only 9%.

There could be various factors driving this downward trend. Including more stay at home orders / stricter social distancing rules. The U.S could be completing less testing, driving the numbers down.
